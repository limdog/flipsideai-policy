Privacy Policy for Flipside AI

Last Updated: July 26, 2025

1. Introduction

This privacy policy outlines how Flipside AI ("we", "our", or "us") uses and protects data when connecting to Google services via our automation platform, hosted at https://workflows.flipsideai.au/.

2. Data We Access

To provide automation services for our clients, our application requires access to certain Google user data. The specific data required depends on the services being automated (e.g., Google Sheets, Gmail, Google Drive). We only request the minimum necessary permissions ("scopes") to perform the designated tasks for our clients.

3. How We Use Data

Google user data is used exclusively to execute the automated workflows configured for our clients. This includes reading, writing, and modifying data in the connected Google services as required by the workflow's logic. Authentication tokens are stored securely within our n8n instance to maintain the connection with Google's servers.

4. How We Share Data

We do not sell or share any Google user data with third parties. All data is handled strictly for the purpose of running the client's configured automations.

5. Adherence to Google Policies

Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at lucas@flipsideai.au.
